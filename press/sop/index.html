About Summer of Pride

The Summer of Pride is a series of LGBTQIA+ (queer) streamers playing queer games, all throughout the month of June. These fun gaymers will be playing some of the best and most unique games that showcase positive representation of queer characters and stories. Tune in and discover some really great games, and follow some really fun streamer personalities.

About MidBoss

Boilerplate

Based out of Berkeley, MidBoss aims to make the world better, safer and more inclusive for marginalized people, especially those across the gender and sexuality spectrum, by creating cool experiences through games and other forms of media which diversify representation, as well as becoming a platform for queer content around the world.
